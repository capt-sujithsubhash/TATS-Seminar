Meteorology &
Regulations

Meteorology
Read forecasts · Identify icing, storms & fog · Make go/no-go decisions
Regulations
EASA airspace · ARROW documentation · ATC procedures · Legal framework
Meteorology outcomes:
Interpret METAR & TAF · Classify cloud hazards · Understand frontal systems
Regulations outcomes:
Navigate EASA airspace · Complete pre-flight docs · Know pilot-in-command responsibilities

Navigation &
Communications

Navigation
Chart reading · Flight planning · VOR/NDB · GPS · Dead reckoning
Communications
ICAO phraseology · ATC procedures · Emergency calls · Radio failure
Navigation outcomes:
Plan cross-country routes · Calculate fuel & time · Read aeronautical charts
Communications outcomes:
Talk to ATC confidently · Correct phraseology · Handle radio failure situations

Principles of Flight

Technical Knowledge
Engines · Fuel · Hydraulics · Electrical · Avionics · Weight & balance
Principles of Flight
Lift · Drag · Thrust · Weight · Stability · Stall & spin recovery
Technical outcomes:
Calculate weight & balance · Understand system failures · Read performance charts
Flight outcomes:
Explain the four forces · Understand stall & recovery · Apply aerodynamics in real flight

Safe Flying

Topics covered:
Situational awareness · Decision-making under pressure · Fatigue management · Crew resource management

REQUIREMENTS

What You Need to
Join TATS

  • 🗓️
    Age
    18+ for Commercial Pilot Licence pathway
  • 📚
    Education
    10+2 with Physics and Mathematics — no university degree required
  • 🎙️
    Screening Interview
    A short interview to understand your motivation and ensure the programme is the right fit for you
  • ✏️
    Written Entrance Exam
    A basic aptitude assessment — tests your logical reasoning and numerical ability, not prior aviation knowledge
  • 🏥
    Aviation Medical
    Class 1 Medical required — we guide you through it, the vast majority of applicants pass
  • 🌐
    English Proficiency
    ICAO Level 4 — if you understand this seminar, you very likely qualify
